# Requesting Access to Non-Public Content

Some content, such as FPGA-compatible models, training scripts, and tools, is not available to the general public.

To access to this content, please [submit a request](mailto:sensaiaccess@latticesemi.com?subject=sensAI%20Access%20Request&body=Full%20name:%0A%0AName%20of%20your%20organization:%0A%0AAddress%20of%20your%20organization:%0A%0AName%20of%20your%20Lattice%20sales%20contact%20or%20other%20sponsor%20(if%20available):%0A%0ASummary%20of%20your%20use%20case%20and/or%20the%20purpose%20for%20which%20you%20intend%20to%20use%20sensAI:%0A%0AWhether%20sensAI%20will%20be%20exported%20to%20a%20U.S.-sanctioned%20country:%0A%0AWhether%20sensAI%20will%20be%20transferred%20or%20re-exported%20to%20another%20country:).

Make sure that you provide the following details to ensure your request is processed promptly:
* Your full name
* Name of your organization
* Address of your organization
* Name of your Lattice sales contact or other sponsor (if available)
* Summary of your use case and/or the purpose for which you intend to use sensAI
* Whether sensAI will be exported to a U.S.-sanctioned country
* Whether sensAI will be transferred or re-exported to another country
